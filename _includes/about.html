About Puck

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Interactive Robotics and Language (IRAL) lab at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County. I am co-advised by Dr. Cynthia Matuszek and Dr. Francis Ferraro.

I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the Colorado School of Mines, advised by Dr. Tom Williams.
My research focuses on robot ethics and linguistic human-robot interaction. I am also interested in emotion understanding and representation and (robotics-centric) Computer Science education.
My overarching research goal is to develop morally and socially competent robots which can exert positive influence and help preserve a better moral ecosystem between humans and robots.
